The Golden Ticket

Place in the top three at a qualifying event, and October in Vegas gets a little cheaper.

Finishing first, second, or third at any Destination Mah Jongg sanctioned tournament earns a Golden Ticket, a $100 discount toward the Mah Jongg World Championship in Las Vegas. Qualifying events are marked with ◆ on the calendar below.
