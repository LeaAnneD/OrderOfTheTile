How the Daily Tile works

Every day, everyone gets the same 13‑tile rack. Your job: figure out which line on the 2026 card this rack is closest to completing — name its section and line number.

You get 5 guesses. After each one Prim tells you:

• how many tiles that line still needs from your rack,
• right section if you picked the correct section,
• 🟨 warm if you're within a tile of the best line, ⬛ cold if it's far.

Find the closest line and your streak grows. Share your result grid — it gives nothing away.

The card is never shown on screen — read your own rack, just like at the table.
