What you watched

The game in the movie is the real game.

All's Fair in Love & Mahjong premiered on the Hallmark Channel in May 2026. It follows a school nurse who finds her way back into community through teaching American Mah Jongg, inspired by the life of Ronni Rice, known to her students as the Marvelous Mrs. Mahj.

The game played in the film is American Mah Jongg, governed by the National Mah Jongg League since 1937. It descends from the older Chinese game, shares the same family of tiles, and then diverges. American Mah Jongg adds Jokers. Uses a yearly card of legal hands published by the League. Has its own Charleston exchange, its own scoring, and its own table manners. Same family. Different table.

The honest answer

Yes, you can actually learn this.

Here is the part the movie does not slow down to tell you. American Mah Jongg looks impossibly complicated for the first ten minutes and then it is not. The tiles number one hundred fifty-two. The hands you can build are listed on a card the League publishes every spring. The Charleston is a series of tile exchanges before play begins. The Joker is wild, but only inside a group of three or more identical tiles.

You can learn the very basics in a three-hour session. The basics of gameplay within about six hours. With regular play, you can be competitive within a few months to a year.

Questions Prim Hears Most

The things everyone asks first.

Is the Mahjong played in All's Fair in Love & Mahjong a real game?
Yes. The film features American Mah Jongg, the variant governed by the National Mah Jongg League since 1937. Same tiles as the older Chinese game, different rules. Hundreds of thousands of people play it across the United States.
How do I start learning American Mah Jongg?
Vocabulary first, the card second, the table third. Start with the Mahjong Glossary. Then read the Card Decoder. Then find a table.
Where do I buy the NMJL card?
Directly from the National Mah Jongg League. The card is published every spring and expires when the next year's card is released. Without the card, American Mah Jongg cannot be played. A warning. Cards sold by non-sanctioned third parties on Amazon and elsewhere have been known to ship inaccurate or counterfeit copies. Buy from the League directly, or from a reseller you trust.
How long does it take to learn?
You can learn the very basics in a three-hour session. The basics of gameplay come within about six hours. With regular play, you can be competitive within a few months to a year. The social game is approachable from day one. The competitive game asks for time and patience.
Is American Mah Jongg different from Chinese Mahjong?
Yes. Both descend from the same nineteenth-century Chinese game and use the same family of tiles, but the rules diverged significantly. American Mah Jongg adds Jokers, uses an annually published card of legal hands, and has its own Charleston exchange and scoring conventions.
Who is the movie based on?
All's Fair in Love & Mahjong is inspired by the real-life journey of Ronni Rice, an American Mah Jongg teacher and enthusiast known as the Marvelous Mrs. Mahj. The film was produced by Joel S. Rice, her husband.
